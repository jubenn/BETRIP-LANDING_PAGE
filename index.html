What is BeTrip?

"We are an application that offers the collective taxi service through a modern platform in which you can navigate and choose your routes to travel quickly, comfortably and, above all, economically."

Our Services

Inside the city ...

When you want to go from one place to another within your city, we offer you the option to do it with other people who go on the same route! All this is possible thanks to the fact that our vehicles are adapted so that more people enter than would enter a normal taxi vehicle, in addition to that you will save money and have the same comfort of traveling in a taxi.

Outside the city ...

Are you planning to leave your city for a few days without spending a lot of money? Well, our option to create routes outside the city and share them with more users is for you. Our platform was also designed for people who wish to hire a vehicle to take them to the outskirts of the city without paying a lot of money, and this is because it will also be a shared ride option and in this way you will not have to go on a bus with too many people, or in a private car that will probably charge you a very high price.
